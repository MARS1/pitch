Service Recipients

What Service Recipients want

  • Convenience: we make it easy for them to post cleaning jobs
  • Security: we let them know who will be cleaning their home
  • Selection: we give them options when choosing a service
  • Flexibility: we allow them to automate and change cleanings
  • Assurance: we ensure the cleaning is done right everytime

Why they want it

  • Busy professionals: some don't have time to shop services
  • Physically incapable: some can't clean their homes
  • Socially Introverted: they don't want to talk on the phone
  • Streamline: they use apps & organize their lives through devices
  • Simplicity: post and wait for biddings on their time

What we do for them

  • Provide a platform: post a job conveniently for free.
  • Provide a cashless payment process: no need to leave cash
  • Allow the market to dictate: by rating their cleaning experience
  • Tracking: see who's cleaning your house and previous cleanings
  • Manage properties: manage cleanings for multiple properties

Service Providers

What Service Providers want

  • Convenience: we make it easy for them to bid cleaning jobs
  • Safety: we let them know who's home they'll be cleaning
  • Selection: post only on the jobs that fit their business needs
  • Flexibility: work only with the customers they want
  • Manageability: easy to manage employees and simple to train on

Why they want it

  • Nothing to lose: no marketing dollars spent on finding customers
  • Manage cleanings easily: manage your jobs through the platform
  • Simple experience: no complicated interface everything is intuitive
  • Time saver: spend less time managing and more time growing
  • Scalable grow your business without worrying how to manage it

What we do for them

  • Provide a platform: to manage cleaning jobs and employees.
  • Centralize payments all funds are sent to the business owner
  • Provide basic analytics: measure avg. cleaning time and more
  • Level the playing field: rate your service recipients as well
  • CRM API intergration: add your favorite services into our platform
